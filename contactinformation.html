Boarding schools and treatment centers are a considerable financial and emotional investment. A decision regarding the best fit for your child into a therapeutic program is a critical, life changing decision. Our firsthand knowledge of schools and programs and our professional and personal experience can guide you to the best possible environment for your child.

We serve families nationwide; having served families from all over the United States, arrangements can be made to meet with families anywhere in the country, and a great deal of consulting work can be done via telephone. The location of your educational consultant is far less important than finding the right "match" for your family.

Please call or email us today to determine if partnering with an educational consultant is right for you.
